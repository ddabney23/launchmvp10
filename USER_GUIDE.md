## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Sign Up** on the homepage
2. Enter your email and create a password (minimum 6 characters)
3. Check your email for verification link
4. Complete your profile setup:
   - Choose a unique username
   - Add a display name
   - Upload a profile picture (optional)
   - Write a short bio (optional)

### First Steps

After signing up, you'll be guided through onboarding:

1. **Select Interests**: Choose categories you're interested in
2. **Profile Setup**: Add your details
3. **Welcome Rewards**: Receive 50 starting credits and 10 points!

## Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

Click your avatar in the top right → **Profile** to view your profile page.

Your profile shows:
- Profile picture and bio
- Posts you've created
- Listings you're selling (if vendor)
- Points, credits, and badges earned
- Followers and following count

### Editing Your Profile

1. Go to your profile
2. Click **Edit Profile**
3. Update your information:
   - Username
   - Display name
   - Bio
   - Avatar
   - Email notifications preferences
4. Click **Save Changes**

## Shopping & Orders

### Browsing Products

1. **Marketplace**: Browse all available products
2. **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific items
3. **Categories**: Filter by category (Food, Clothing, Electronics, etc.)
4. **Vendors**: Browse products from specific vendors

### Adding to Cart

1. Click on a product to view details
2. Review description, price, and images
3. Click **Add to Cart**
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### Checkout Process

1. Click the **Cart icon** in the navigation
2. Review items in your cart
3. Click **Proceed to Checkout**
4. Enter shipping information
5. Choose payment method
6. Enter payment details
7. Click **Complete Order**

### Payment Options

We accept:
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

### Tracking Orders

1. Go to **Orders** in the menu
2. View all your orders and their status:
   - **Pending**: Order placed, awaiting payment
   - **Paid**: Payment received, preparing shipment
   - **Shipped**: Order on its way
   - **Delivered**: Order completed
   - **Canceled**: Order canceled
   - **Refunded**: Payment refunded

3. Click an order to view full details

## Bookings

### Making a Booking

1. Find a bookable listing (services, rentals, experiences)
2. Click on the listing
3. Click **Book Now**
4. Select dates using the calendar
5. Add any special requests in notes
6. Review booking details and total price
7. Click **Request Booking**

### Booking Status

- **Pending**: Waiting for vendor confirmation
- **Confirmed**: Vendor approved your booking
- **Completed**: Booking finished
- **Canceled**: Booking was canceled

### Managing Bookings

View your bookings in **My Bookings**:
- See upcoming bookings
- View past bookings
- Cancel bookings (if allowed)
- Leave reviews after completion

## Social Features

### Creating Posts

1. Click **Create Post** in the navigation
2. Write your message (up to 2000 characters)
3. Add images or videos (optional)
4. Choose visibility:
   - **Public**: Everyone can see
   - **Followers**: Only followers see
   - **Private**: Only you see
5. Click **Post**

### Engaging with Content

- **Like**: Click the heart icon
- **Comment**: Share your thoughts
- **Share**: Spread posts you love
- **Save**: Bookmark for later

### Following Users

1. Visit a user's profile
2. Click **Follow**
3. See their posts in your feed

### Messages

Send direct messages to other users:

1. Go to **Messages**
2. Click **New Message**
3. Select recipient
4. Type your message
5. Click **Send**

### Groups

Join communities around shared interests:

1. Go to **Groups**
2. Browse available groups
3. Click **Join** on groups you like
4. Participate in group discussions

## Becoming a Vendor

### Vendor Application

1. Go to **Settings** → **Become a Vendor**
2. Fill out the application:
   - Business name
   - Business category
   - Business ID/registration (optional)
   - Tax ID (optional)
   - Business description
3. Submit application
4. Wait for admin approval (usually 1-3 business days)

### Creating Listings

Once approved as a vendor:

1. Go to **Vendor Dashboard**
2. Click **Create Listing**
3. Fill in details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Price
   - Category
   - Images (up to 5)
   - Stock quantity
4. Click **Publish**

### Managing Orders

In your Vendor Dashboard:

- View all incoming orders
- Update order status
- Communicate with customers
- Process refunds if needed

### Vendor Analytics

Track your performance:
- Total sales
- Order count
- Revenue trends
- Top selling products
- Customer reviews

## Account Security

### Password Management

**Change Password**:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (twice)
5. Click **Update Password**

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add extra security:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes securely
6. Click **Enable**

### Active Sessions

Manage logged-in devices:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. View **Active Sessions**
3. Click **Sign Out** on unknown devices

### Account Deletion

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Confirm by entering your password
4. All data will be permanently deleted

⚠️ **Warning**: This action cannot be undone!

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In

- **Forgot password**: Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- **Email not verified**: Check spam folder for verification email
- **Account locked**: Contact support@optimix.com

### Payment Failed

- **Card declined**: Check with your bank
- **Insufficient funds**: Add funds to your account
- **Expired card**: Update payment method
- **Technical issue**: Try again or contact support

### Order Issues

- **Wrong item received**: Contact vendor through order page
- **Item not received**: Check tracking or contact vendor
- **Damaged item**: Request refund/replacement
- **Late delivery**: Track order or message vendor

### Booking Problems

- **Booking not confirmed**: Wait for vendor response
- **Want to cancel**: Check cancellation policy
- **Date conflict**: Contact vendor to reschedule

### Technical Issues

- **Page not loading**: Refresh browser or clear cache
- **Images not uploading**: Check file size (max 5MB)
- **App crashing**: Update to latest version
- **Features not working**: Try different browser
